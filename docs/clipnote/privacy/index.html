Privacy Policy for ClipNote

Last Updated: October 22, 2025

Introduction

ClipNote ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our meeting transcription and summary application.

Information We Collect

Audio and Video Recordings

  • Audio recordings from meetings you choose to record
  • Video recordings (if applicable) from meetings you upload
  • Transcripts you manually paste into the app

Meeting Data

  • Meeting titles you create
  • AI-generated transcriptions of your audio/video
  • AI-generated summaries, action items, and key insights
  • Timestamps and metadata associated with your meetings

Device Information

  • Device identifiers for authentication and app functionality
  • Device model and operating system version
  • App version and usage analytics

Payment Information

  • Subscription tier information (Free, Professional, or Business)
  • Payment transactions are processed through Google Play Store and Apple App Store
  • We do not directly collect or store credit card information

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Transcribe your audio and video recordings using AI technology
  • Generate meeting summaries, action items, and key insights
  • Provide cloud storage for paid tier users
  • Process and manage your subscription
  • Improve our app's functionality and user experience
  • Provide customer support

Camera Permission

Our app requests camera permission to allow you to:

  • Record video during meetings (optional feature)
  • Capture visual content for meeting documentation

You can use ClipNote without granting camera access. The core audio recording and transcription features work independently of camera permissions.

Data Storage and Retention

Free Tier Users

  • Meetings are stored locally on your device
  • Limited to 5 meetings per month
  • You control deletion through your device storage

Paid Tier Users (Professional & Business)

  • Meetings are stored on secure cloud servers
  • Automatic deletion after 90 days from the date of upload
  • Faster processing and unlimited meeting storage during the 90-day period

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission (SSL/TLS)
  • Secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Restricted access to personal information

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • AI Processing: Audio/video is processed through third-party AI services for transcription and analysis
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Your Rights and Choices

Data Deletion Requests

  • Users can request deletion of their data through the in-app contact form
  • Cloud-stored data is automatically deleted after 90 days
  • We will respond to deletion requests within 30 days

Access to Your Data

  • You can view all your meetings and transcripts within the app
  • You can request a copy of your data by contacting us

Opt-Out Options

  • You can deny camera permissions in your device settings
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to stop data collection

Children's Privacy

ClipNote is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • AI Transcription Services: For converting audio to text
  • Cloud Storage Providers: For paid tier data storage
  • Payment Processors: Google Play Store and Apple App Store for subscriptions

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification through the app (for material changes)

Your continued use of ClipNote after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through the ClipNote app.

In-App Contact: Go to Settings → Privacy Policy → Contact Us

We will respond to all inquiries within 30 days.

Your Consent

By using ClipNote, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Note for Business Tier Users: Additional terms may apply regarding team collaboration features, admin controls, and multi-user access. Please refer to your Business subscription agreement for details.
